# SMS Compliance & Consent Information

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

This page provides information about FilterFive's SMS messaging practices and consent requirements for compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), Cellular Telecommunications Industry Association (CTIA) guidelines, and carrier requirements.

---

## About FilterFive SMS Messages

FilterFive is a business-to-business platform that helps local businesses request customer reviews via SMS. When you receive an SMS from a FilterFive-powered business, it means:

1. You recently visited or did business with a local business
2. That business uses FilterFive to request customer feedback
3. You provided your phone number and consent to receive a review request

---

## Message Details

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Message Type** | One-time transactional review request |
| **Frequency** | Maximum ONE message per 30-day period per business |
| **Content** | Brief thank-you message with a link to leave a Google review |
| **Cost** | Message and data rates may apply based on your carrier plan |

---

## Sample Message

Here is an example of messages sent through FilterFive:

```
Hi [Name]! Thanks for visiting [Business Name]. We'd love your feedback: 
https://g.page/r/xxxxx/review Msg&data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.
```

---

## How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying with any of these keywords:

- **STOP**
- **UNSUBSCRIBE**
- **CANCEL**
- **END**
- **QUIT**

Opt-out requests are processed **immediately and automatically**. Once you opt out, you will not receive any further messages.

---

## How to Get Help

Reply **HELP** to any message to receive:

```
FilterFive sends review requests on behalf of local businesses. 
For help, contact info@filterfive.io or visit filterfive.io/sms-compliance. 
Reply STOP to opt out.
```

You can also contact us directly at **info@filterfive.io**.

## Consent Requirements for Businesses

FilterFive requires all subscribing businesses to obtain **prior express written consent** before submitting any customer phone numbers. This section documents our consent requirements.

### Required Consent Elements

All consent collection must include:

1. **Business Identification** — Clear identification of the business name
2. **Message Description** — Clear statement that the customer will receive an SMS review request
3. **Frequency Disclosure** — Statement that this is a one-time message
4. **Rate Disclosure** — "Message and data rates may apply"
5. **Opt-Out Instructions** — "Reply STOP to opt out"
6. **Policy Links** — References to terms of service and privacy policy

### Consent Record Requirements

Businesses must maintain records of consent including:

| Required Element | Description |
|-----------------|-------------|
| **Timestamp** | Date and time consent was obtained |
| **Method** | How consent was collected (web form, paper, etc.) |
| **Consent Text** | The exact language shown to the customer |
| **Acknowledgment** | Proof of customer action (checkbox selected, signature, etc.) |
| **Retention** | Records must be kept for minimum 4 years |

## Compliance Verification

FilterFive enforces compliance through:

1. **Contractual Obligation** — Businesses agree to consent requirements in our [Terms of Service](/terms)
2. **30-Day Frequency Limit** — System prevents messages to the same number within 30 days
3. **Automatic Opt-Out Processing** — STOP requests are honored immediately across all businesses
4. **Audit Rights** — FilterFive may request consent documentation from businesses
